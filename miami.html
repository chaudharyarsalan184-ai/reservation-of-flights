Miami, a city in Florida, is known for its nightlife. If you want to escape the rush of town, a Miami holiday maybe is just what you need. Those who love being near water must be familiar with ‘Lummus Park Beach.’ There’s no shortage of gorgeous views to soak up while you’re there in Miami. It has multiple enticements for everyone- from the trendy nightlife of South Beach to the historic hideaways of Coral Gables.


Must-Do things in Miami:

Spend the day in a Private Yacht: Climb aboard a breathtakingly gorgeous yacht, and party like you’ve got nothing to lose. Have a customized party experience on a yacht-like you’re in a movie, and you’ve got money to blow.


Grab a drink Casa Florida: Casa Florida is a vintage cocktail house set up in the historic Miami River Inn. The cold kitchen offers seafood and vegetarian options and brunch specials during the weekend; you can sit and sip inside your retro-style bus surrounded by everything.


Enjoy Drag Queen brunches: Brunches have never been this diverting. Sip on bottomless mimosas with Miss Ultimate Miami Drag Queen for an excellent boozy brunch along with exquisite performances through the day from Miami’s most fabulous drag performers.


Best time to visit miami:

For lesser tourist's footfall and a pleasant climate, the best time to visit Miami is between November and March. The weather has cooled slightly and you can expect less rainfall.
