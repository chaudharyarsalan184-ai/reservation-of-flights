Top Tourist Attractions in New York:

The Statue of Liberty stands as one of America’s most recognizable landmarks, rising 152 feet from base to torch and weighing nearly 450,000 pounds. A gift from France in 1886, it has since become an enduring symbol of freedom and democracy. Today, it continues to be one of New York City’s most visited and celebrated tourist attractions.


Central Park is a must-visit on any New York City itinerary, offering countless ways to explore its scenic pathways—whether by walking, cycling, or enjoying a classic carriage ride. In winter, visitors can lace up their skates and glide across the iconic Wollman Rink. Beyond its natural beauty, the park is home to several beloved attractions, including Belvedere Castle, Strawberry Fields, the Central Park Zoo, and the picturesque lake, making it an experience unlike any other.
