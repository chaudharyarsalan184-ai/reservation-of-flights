About Moscow

Moscow, Russia’s vibrant capital, blends centuries of history with modern culture, from the iconic Red Square to the dazzling domes of St. Basil’s Cathedral. It’s a city of art, architecture, and rich traditions, offering visitors both grandeur and charm.


Top Attractions

The Kremlin & Red Square- Moscow’s heart and a UNESCO World Heritage site, featuring stunning cathedrals, government buildings, and the famed Ivan the Great Bell Tower .

St. Basil’s Cathedral – A striking symbol of Russia with its rainbow onion domes. Initially white, it gained color in the 17th century inspired by Biblical motifs

Bolshoi Theatre – A world-renowned venue for ballet and opera. A must-see for performing arts lovers.


Best Time to Visit

Spring offers the perfect balance of pleasant weather, modest crowds, and better prices.


Local Cuisine

From borscht and pelmeni to beef stroganoff, blini, and savory pastries, Moscow’s culinary offerings are as rich and diverse as its history.
